 <h1>Big-Basket Ecommerce "CLONE" </h1>
 
 <h3>Description :</h3>
 Big Basket is an Indian Online grocery and household store with wide range of products.
 This was a collaborative project assigned to us at Masai School during the construct week. We were a team of 5 members and completed the project in 1 week. 
 
 <h3>My Area of Responsibility</h3>
 1. built product page <br>
 2. built single product page <br>
 3. product fetch functionality using Axios for the above 2 pages <br>
 4. Product filter and sort functionality by price and rating <br>
 5. Data persisting through react-redux and useSearchParams, useLocation
